About 101Headshots

Founded in 2015, 101Headshots emerged from a vision to revolutionize professional photography in Mission, Kansas. Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical session take?

Our standard sessions last 30-45 minutes, allowing time for multiple poses and outfit changes. This includes a brief consultation to understand your goals and style preferences.

When will I receive my photos?

You'll receive your professionally edited photos within 48 hours through our secure online gallery. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

What should I wear to my session?

We recommend solid colors and professional attire that aligns with your industry. We'll provide detailed guidance during your pre-session consultation.

Do you offer retouching services?

Yes, professional retouching is included with every session. We focus on natural-looking results that enhance your appearance while maintaining authenticity.

We understand that plans change. You can reschedule your session up to 48 hours before your appointment at no additional cost.
