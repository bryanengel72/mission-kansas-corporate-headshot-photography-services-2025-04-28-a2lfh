How to Prepare for Your Corporate Headshot Session

April 28, 2025

A professional headshot is a crucial investment in your personal brand and career success. Proper preparation begins days before the session with adequate rest and skincare. Choose solid-colored clothing that complements your skin tone and aligns with industry standards, avoiding busy patterns or trendy styles. Professional grooming is essential - men should get a timely haircut, while women should opt for natural-looking makeup. During the session, maintain good posture and trust your photographer's expertise with lighting and positioning. Remember that your expression should balance professionalism with authenticity, as this headshot will often serve as your first impression.
